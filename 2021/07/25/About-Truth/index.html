2021-07-25
2021-07-25

Math is the highest subject among all the subjects taught in the school, it is the only subject about truth, ordinary human beings can share at least some divinity by studying mathmatics.

Subjects like physics, chemistry and history. These are great intelligence crystallizations by humans, but they are only tools that help us to attain a better life, they are not truth. Like beautiful sickles made by a blacksmith with his exquisite skills that allows us to harvest foods with multiplied efficiency compared to hands. We praise the greatness of the sickle, but sickles are not truth.

There is only one science of the highest intelligence – the all-encompassing science.The science of elucidating the entire universe and the place of human life in it. (War and Peace, Pg 398.)

“Amicus Plato, amicus Aristotle, sed magis amica Veritas.”

在所有的学科当中，数学是顶神圣的，唯一的真理学科，通过学习数学人类得以分享神性。

物理、化学、历史，这些人们奉为圭臬的智力结晶，不过是工具，使人们过得更好的工具。就像一名铁匠用精巧的技艺打造出了一把漂亮的镰刀，这把镰刀可以使我们以相较于手工成倍的效率收割食物，我们赞美镰刀的伟大，但镰刀不是真理。

最高智慧只有一种科学—包罗万象的科学。阐明整个宇宙以及人生在其中所占地位的科学。

与柏拉图为友，与亚里士多德为友，最重要的是与真理为友
